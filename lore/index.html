The Emperor's Powerbase

In the Sith Brotherhood, the Emperor's powerbase is the core structure that sustains his rule and projects his will throughout the organization. A powerbase in Sith culture refers to a collective of resources, individuals, and networks loyal to a single leader.

While the Dark Council governs broader aspects of the Brotherhood, the powerbase ensures its internal efficiency and alignment with the Emperor's will. These individuals are handpicked by the Emperor himself, embodying the pinnacle of Sith ideology.
