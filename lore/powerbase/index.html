The Powerbase

The Synergy of the Powerbase

The Emperor’s powerbase is a testament to the Sith principle of strength through unity. Each member fulfills a unique role, yet their efforts are tightly coordinated to ensure the Brotherhood operates as a single, indomitable force.

This synergy allows the Emperor to maintain an iron grip over the Sith Brotherhood while focusing on his ascension as the Dark Side Entity’s chosen vessel. Through the efforts of his powerbase, the Emperor’s will echoes across the galaxy.

The Voice of the Emperor

Secondary Emperor and Assistant

The Voice of the Emperor acts as the Emperor’s most trusted advisor and mouthpiece, speaking and acting in his stead when necessary. The Voice is the only individual, aside from the Emperor, allowed to commune directly with the Dark Side Entity.

When the Emperor is absent, the Voice assumes full authority, ruling as a proxy with the same unchallengeable dominion. Their mastery of Force abilities often rivals that of the Emperor himself, ensuring their capacity to enforce his will without question.

The Wrath of the Emperor

Division Manager

The Wrath of the Emperor serves as the enforcer of the Brotherhood, overseeing all divisions and ensuring their operations align with the Emperor’s strategic vision. The Wrath embodies the Emperor’s rage and serves as his personal hammer, bringing swift judgment to those who falter or defy his will.

As the overseer of all divisions, the Wrath directly manages communication between the Emperor’s powerbase, the Dark Council, and the Brotherhood’s broader ranks. In battle, the Wrath is a terrifying force, leading from the front with unmatched ferocity.

The Hand of the Emperor

Dark Council Overseer

The Hand of the Emperor acts as the liaison and overseer of the Dark Council, ensuring its members remain aligned with the Emperor’s vision and do not succumb to petty rivalries or personal ambition.

The Hand is both a mediator and a judge, wielding the authority to enforce discipline among even the most powerful Sith Lords. They are tasked with monitoring the performance of each Council member, addressing inefficiencies, and ensuring their actions serve the Brotherhood’s greater goals.
