The Dark Council

The Supreme Authority

At the apex of the Sith Brotherhood's hierarchy stands the Dark Council, a body of the most powerful and cunning Sith Lords in the galaxy. These masters serve as the Emperor's advisors, commanders, and executors of his will. The Council operates under a strict hierarchy where each member oversees specific spheres of influence.

Darth Aelthel — Law and Doctrine

Arbiter of the Brotherhood’s legal and doctrinal framework. He upholds the Emperor’s decrees and enforces the Sith Code. To Aelthel, law is a weapon used to maintain order and crush dissent. Disputes among Sith Lords are brought before his cold, methodical judgment in the Great Temple.

Darth Cassian — Human Resources and Trials

Manages the Brotherhood’s hierarchy, focusing on High and Mid-Rank efficiency. A master tactician who ensures leadership maintains quotas for training and recruitment. He facilitates trials and progression, tolerating no incompetence while advocating for those who exceed his brutal standards.

Darth Magnus — Diplomacy and Alliances

Head of the Division of Recruitment and Rank Management (DRRM). Magnus excels at identifying talent, enforcing loyalty, and molding raw recruits into efficient operatives. He balances administrative rigor with strategic guidance to ensure the Brotherhood’s foundation remains solid.

Darth Novius — Morale and Innovation

Overseer of cohesion and motivation. Novius organizes grand combat tournaments and victory celebrations to inspire loyalty and foster healthy competition. His innovative initiatives bridge the gap between acolytes and high-ranking Sith Lords, keeping the order active and engaged.

Darth Stryker — Raids and Warfare

Supreme commander of military campaigns. Stryker coordinates devastating assaults against Jedi temples and Republic outposts. A warrior to his core, he often leads from the front, striking with a combination of precision and overwhelming brutality that has made him a legend.

Darth Umbral — The Inquisitorius

Commander of the Inquisitorius (IQ). Dedicated to hunting Jedi, rooting out heresy, and preserving dark side purity. Umbral is a master of Sith sorcery and alchemy, deploying agents across the galaxy to eliminate any threat to the Brotherhood’s spiritual supremacy.

Darth Valerian — Intelligence and Espionage

Leader of Imperial Intelligence (II). Valerian operates in the shadows with a vast network of spies and assassins. His ability to anticipate enemy movements through surgical information gathering ensures the Brotherhood remains one step ahead of the Republic and the Jedi.

Darth Aegis — Dark Honor Guard

Overseer of the Dark Honor Guard (DHG). Sworn protector of the Emperor and the Dark Council. Aegis is a paragon of discipline, personally training the Brotherhood’s most loyal warriors to serve as the shield of the Dark Side Entity’s conduit.

Darth Vorlag — The Reavers

Head of the Reavers (REV), the Brotherhood’s elite assassins. Vorlag is a master of stealth and psychological manipulation, using his agents to destabilize governments and dismantle rival factions from within. He works closely with Valerian to ensure total control over the flow of chaos.

The Council Chamber & Rituals

Deep within the Great Temple lies the Council Chamber, where twelve ornate thrones surround the Emperor's seat. The Council convenes monthly during the new moon to perform dark rituals, blood offerings, and strategic planning, shaping the destiny of thousands across the galaxy.
