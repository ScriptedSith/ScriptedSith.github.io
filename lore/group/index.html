Chronicles of the Brotherhood

Origins on Korriban

The tale of the Sith Brotherhood starts on the desolate and awe-inspiring world of Korriban, with crimson dunes and looming tombs of ancient Sith Lords whispering secrets of unbridled power. It was here, in the cradle of the Sith species, that the Emperor rebuilt the Order.

Unlike other Sith Lords whose ambition often led to infighting, the Emperor preached unity for the sake of total domination. Korriban became a proving ground; recruits were pushed ruthlessly in the Valley of the Dark Lords. Only the strongest emerged as Sith Knights and Lords, loyal only to the Emperor's dream.

The Emperor’s Ascension

Two months into the Brotherhood's life, the fate of the Sith reordered: the Emperor, deep in meditation in the Tomb of Marka Ragnos, underwent a transformation unique in history. Pulsating with power, he exited the tomb as a Dark Side Entity—a being beyond mortality.

This primordial consciousness granted him knowledge beyond comprehension in return for serving as its vessel. Loyalty shifted from the mundane ambitions of a man to the cosmic whims of the Dark Side itself.

The Move to Yavin IV

The Entity bestowed a vision: Yavin IV, a jungle moon bathed in dark side energy, was to be their new home. Despite the harsh attachment to Korriban's sands, the Brotherhood moved en-masse to the ancient Massassi temples.

The moon proved as deadly as it was resplendent. Sith alchemists and sorcerers adapted the ruins into fortresses and academies, bending the chaotic jungle energy to the Emperor's absolute will.

The Entity’s Influence

The Emperor often retreated into solitude to commune with the Entity, returning with prophetic decrees. Sacrificial rituals of blood and vital energy became frequent in the Great Temple.

Fleets launched from secret shipyards struck Republic outposts and Jedi enclaves, establishing dark side nexuses across the galaxy to amplify the Entity's presence.

Life in the Era of Yavin IV

Life is a daily struggle for power. Sith Lords oversee brutal training while alchemists experiment with Sithspawn.

Under Darth Malgus, Yavin IV serves as a staging ground for raids. Meanwhile, Darth Acina ensures the Brotherhood's unity through great ceremonies and promotions, keeping the lower ranks focused and disciplined.

Late Days & The Future

The Sith Brotherhood is now a monolith of terror. While the Emperor serves as a vessel for the Entity, whispers of dissent linger among those who fear being mere pawns in a cosmic game.

A storm brews in the galaxy, but the Brotherhood readies to embrace it, convinced that the darkness shall prevail.
