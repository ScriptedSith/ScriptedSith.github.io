〔The Sith Brotherhood〕

The Sith Brotherhood was a sect of Force-sensitives who utilized the dark side of the Force. The Sith were the most infamous of all dark side practices, and the members of the order were often seen as the pinnacle of power within the dark side. A force not to challenge, forged from militarism and fueled by the almighty power of the dark side of the Force. United by the supreme master, the Emperor and the Dark Council.
