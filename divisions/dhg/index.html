Dark Honor Guard

The Dark Honor Guard is a force of elite warriors who serve the Sith Emperor of the reconstituted Sith Empire as his personal protectors and enforcers. Silent, disciplined, and lethally efficient, they are the final line of defense for the Imperial Throne.
