Inquisitors

Sith Inquisitors are a class of powerful Force-wielders operating within the upper echelons of the Empire. Specializing in Force manipulation and ancient knowledge rather than raw martial power, they are masters of the cutthroat political environment that defines the Sith.

"The true power of the dark side; it is claimed by many, but known by few."
— Darth Nox
