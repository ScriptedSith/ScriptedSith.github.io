〔𝐈𝐧𝐪𝐮𝐢𝐬𝐢𝐭𝐨𝐫𝐬〕

Sith Inquisitors were a class of powerful Force-wielders within the reconstituted Sith Empire, operating primarily within the upper echelons and political circles. Inquisitors specialized in Force abilities as opposed to martial might, and many would devote much time to researching new skills to survive in the cutthroat Sith political environment.
