Trainings

Combat Training

Start off by telling them who you are and your rank, introduce your co-host if you have one.
Have them whisper the Sith code line by line.
Duel each other 3 times.
TDM or FFA or Health Rotations
Then finish the training, log it, then pass them if there is any that passed.

Lore Training

Start off by telling them who you are and your rank, introduce your co-host if you have one.
Have them whisper the Sith code line by line.
Pick a subject in The Sith Holocron area and teach it.
Then do a 5 question Quiz, must get 3 to pass.
Then finish the training, log it, then pass them if there is any that passed.

Law Training

Start off by telling them who you are and your rank, introduce your co-host if you have one.
Have them whisper the Sith code line by line.
Go over 3 sections of the Imperial Policy.
Then do a 5 question Quiz, must get 3 to pass.
Then finish the training, log it, then pass them if there is any that passed.
