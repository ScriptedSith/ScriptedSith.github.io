Trials

Apprentice Trial

Sith Apprentices may attend their master hosting these trials so long as they have been their apprentice for 1 week minimum.
Have them whisper the Sith code line by line.
The Master must inform the apprentice of what the trial will consist of. The master may choose how they want to run it.
Once the apprentice passes the trial they are ranked Sith Knight in the group and may choose whether they wish to be your apprentice anymore.

Knight Trial

Sith Eradicators can pass this trial in order to become an NCO and learn the jobs of being an HR and can be hosted by a Sith Master+ and consists of:
Recite the Sith Code, with no mistakes.
Answer 2 Lore Questions, may get 1 wrong.
Answer 3 IP Questions, may get 1 wrong.
Duel the host 3 times.
Upon the event completion and them being ranked and recorded. Announce KOS for 3 minutes.
Host has complete authority to pass or fail attendees based on combat or how the attendee acts during the trial.

Overseer Trial

Overseer Trials may be hosted by a Sith Lord+ and consist of:
Recite the Sith Code, with no mistakes.
Answer 5 Lore Questions, may get 2 wrong.
Answer 5 IP Questions, may get 2 wrong.
Duel the host 7 times.
Upon the event completion and them being ranked and recorded. Announce KOS for 5 minutes.
Host has complete authority to pass or fail attendees based on combat or how the attendee acts during the trial.

Darth Trial

To become a Darth, you must request to be voted to the rank and gain 2/3 votes by the Dark Council+. The trial consists of:
Recite the Sith Code, with no mistakes.
Answer 10 Lore Questions, may get none wrong.
Answer 10 IP Questions, may get none wrong.
Duel the host203 times.
Upon the event completion and them being ranked and recorded. Announce KOS for 15 minutes.
Host has complete authority to pass or fail attendees based on combat or how the attendee acts during the trial.
