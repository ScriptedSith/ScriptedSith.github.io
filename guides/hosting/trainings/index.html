Training Protocols

Combat Training

  • Introduce yourself, your rank, and any co-hosts.
  • Require attendees to whisper the Sith Code line by line.
  • Conduct standard duels (Best of 3).
  • Execute a specialized combat phase: Team Deathmatch (TDM), Free-For-All (FFA), or Health Rotations.
  • Conclude the session, log the results, and promote those who displayed sufficient skill.

Lore Training

  • Introduce yourself, your rank, and any co-hosts.
  • Require attendees to whisper the Sith Code line by line.
  • Select a specific subject from the Sith Holocron and provide a detailed lesson.
  • Administer a 5-question quiz (Attendees must score at least 3/5 to pass).
  • Conclude the session, log the results, and promote successful scholars.

Law Training

  • Introduce yourself, your rank, and any co-hosts.
  • Require attendees to whisper the Sith Code line by line.
  • Explain 3 critical sections of the Imperial Policy.
  • Administer a 5-question quiz (Attendees must score at least 3/5 to pass).
  • Conclude the session, log the results, and promote those who demonstrate legal competence.
