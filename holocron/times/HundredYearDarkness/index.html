The Hundred Year Darkness

The Second Great Schism

The Hundred Year Darkness, more commonly known as the Second Great Schism, began when rogue Jedi Knights sought to master the art of shaping life itself. Experimenting with the creation of new species, these masters—Ajunta Pall, XoXaan, and Karness Muur—believed their quest for eternal life was the true path of the Force.

Exiled from the Jedi Order, they raised an army of dark side abominations to strike back at their former brothers. Though they unleashed terrifying Leviathans capable of consuming life essence, the Jedi eventually cornered the Exiles on Corbos. Following their surrender, the Dark Jedi were stripped of their weapons and banished into the unknown reaches of space.

On an uncharted world, the Exiles discovered the Sith species. After deposing the local ruler, Hakagram Graush, the Dark Jedi ascended to godhood among the natives. This era accelerated the cultural development of the Sith, merging the identity of the species with the title of the fallen Jedi Masters, marking the true birth of the Sith Empire.
