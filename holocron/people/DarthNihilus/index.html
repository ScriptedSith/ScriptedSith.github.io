Darth Nihilus

Lord of Hunger

A survivor of the activation of the Mass Shadow Generator at Malachor V, the man who would become Darth Nihilus was transformed into a "wound in the Force." Consumed by an endless hunger for Force energy, he was discovered by Darth Traya and trained at the Trayus Academy alongside Darth Sion.

Together, they formed the Sith Triumvirate. Nihilus and Sion eventually betrayed their master, stripping Traya of her power and casting her into exile. As Nihilus' hunger grew, his physical form began to erode, forcing him to call upon the dark side to encase his spirit within his iconic mask and armor to survive.

Nihilus was responsible for the total devastation of the planet Katarr in 3952 BBY, where he consumed the life essence of every living thing on the world, leaving only Visas Marr alive to serve as his Shadow Hand. His flagship, the Ravager, became a beacon of dread as he scoured the galaxy during the First Jedi Purge.

His reign ended during the Battle of Telos IV, where he was confronted by the Jedi Exile, Meetra Surik. In a final clash of wills and blades aboard his dying ship, Nihilus was slain. His spirit remains tethered to his armor, which was collected for burial on the Sith homeworld of Korriban.
