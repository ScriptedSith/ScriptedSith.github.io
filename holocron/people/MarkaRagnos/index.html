Marka Ragnos

Lord of the Golden Age

A Sith-Human hybrid and direct descendant of the original Dark Jedi exiles, Marka Ragnos was the most feared and admired Dark Lord of his age. Rising to power circa 5100 BBY, he secured his mantle by defeating his rival, Simus, in a formidable duel that proved his absolute worthiness to lead the Empire.

His iron-fisted rule lasted over a century, a period defined by ruthless internal stability. Ragnos was a master manipulator who pitted his adversaries against one another to ensure no threat ever reached his throne. During this time, he crafted legendary artifacts, including a Scepter capable of siphoning and storing Force energy and Gauntlets designed to augment the physical and metaphysical strength of the wearer.

It was Ragnos who first recognized the potential of the young Tenebrae. Impressed by the thirteen-year-old's cold ambition and raw power, Ragnos granted him the name Darth Vitiate and recognized him as the ruler of Medriaas, setting the stage for the next great era of Sith power.

Marka Ragnos passed away in 5000 BBY. His death signaled the end of the Sith Empire’s "Golden Age" and triggered the chaotic power struggle between Naga Sadow and Ludo Kressh that would eventually lead to the Great Hyperspace War.
