King Adas

The Original Sith'ari

Adas was the king of Korriban and the Sith in a legendary reign that spanned nearly three hundred years. Distinguishable by his unusual charcoal-colored skin and massive stature, he was revered as a chosen being from his youth.

Encased in ebon armor and wielding a colossal battle-axe, Adas became the undisputed master of Sith magic. Around 28,000 BBY, he unified the warring nations of Korriban through a series of bloody conflicts, solidifying his role as the sole leader of his people. The Sith hailed him as the Sith'ari, believing his invincibility would ensure an eternal reign.

Circa 27,700 BBY, the Rakata of the Infinite Empire arrived on Korriban. While they initially shared the technology of holocrons with Adas, he quickly realized their true intent was conquest. Adas led his people in a fierce counter-offensive to drive the invaders from Korriban, a victory that cost him his life but secured the freedom of the Sith.

His legacy endured through his holocron, which was eventually granted to the Dark Jedi Exiles in 6,900 BBY. This artifact passed through the hands of historical titans, eventually being discovered by the fallen Jedi Freedon Nadd, who utilized Adas's ancient teachings to establish his own dark dominion over Onderon.
