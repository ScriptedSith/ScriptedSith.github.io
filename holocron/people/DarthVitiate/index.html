Darth Vitiate

The Immortal Emperor

Born in 5113 BBY as Tenebrae, the illegitimate son of Lord Dramath, he carved a path of slaughter across Medriaas before he was even a teenager. After imprisoning his father's spirit for eternity, he was christened Lord Vitiate by Marka Ragnos. Following the Great Hyperspace War, Vitiate orchestrated a dark ritual on Nathema, consuming the life essence of the entire planet and every Sith Lord present to grant himself immortality.

Reborn as the Sith Emperor, he led survivors to Dromund Kaas to rebuild in secret. Over the next millennium, his consciousness expanded, leading him to create the Eternal Empire on Zakuul under the name Valkorion. He viewed the Sith Empire and the Galactic Republic merely as fuel for his ultimate goal: a galaxy-wide ritual to transform himself into an all-powerful god.

His reign was challenged by legendary figures like Revan, whose mental war delayed the Emperor's plans for centuries. Despite host bodies being destroyed by the Hero of Tython and his spirit being nearly resurrected on Yavin IV, Vitiate remained a cosmic threat, eventually consuming the world of Ziost to return to full strength.

In his final gambit as Valkorion, he sought to use the Outlander as a vessel for his supreme power. However, through a final clash of wills involving his own family, the ancient entity was finally banished into the void, ending a multi-millennial reign of terror.
