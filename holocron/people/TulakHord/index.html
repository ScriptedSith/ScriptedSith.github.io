Tulak Hord

Lord of Hate

A Force-sensitive male, Tulak Hord was a master of the dark side and a pupil of the early Sith Empire. Rising through the ranks, Hord was feared by his rivals even in his early days, having mastered the art of lightsaber combat and manipulation of the Force.

During his early years, Hord fought an honorable duel against a Dashade named Khem Val, who later became his most faithful ally. Assembling a massive army, Hord and Val led a campaign of terror across Yn and Chabosh. Despite dismissing him at first, Hord eventually accepted Aloysius Kallig into his ranks after Kallig defeated Hord’s best general, leading the Empire to numerous victories.

Undefeated in battle, Hord earned the titles Master of the Gathering Darkness and Dark Lord of the Sith. He was notorious for employing Sith rituals that drained the strength of his opponents to feed his own power. However, his fear of rivals led him to assassinate Kallig, forcing Kallig’s family into hiding.

Beyond the battlefield, Lord Hord was a master of Sith sorcery, authoring many tomes and crafting his personal Holocron. He is rumored to have conquered over a hundred worlds and discovered the Dromund system.

His reign ended in betrayal when his only known apprentice, Ortan Cela, stabbed him through the back. Following his death, his minions constructed a massive tomb in the Valley of the Dark Lords on Korriban, where his mask and holocron remain entombed.
