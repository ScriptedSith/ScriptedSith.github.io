Naga Sadow

Alchemist of the Empire

Naga Sadow was a master Sith alchemist and Dark Lord of the Sith who, in 5000 BBY, took the Sith Empire to the brink of galactic domination in the Great Hyperspace War. A half-breed Sith from Ziost, Sadow ruled over a secret citadel on the moon Khar Shian, where he became one of the most powerful Sith magicians of his time.

Following the death of Marka Ragnos, Sadow engaged in a brutal power struggle with his conservative rival, Ludo Kressh. Sadow used the arrival of Republic explorers Gav and Jori Daragon to foment fear and justify an expansionist war. After shattering Kressh's forces at Khar Delba, Sadow unified the Sith military for a massive surprise attack on the Republic.

During the invasion, Sadow directed his fleets from a Sith meditation sphere, using his sorcery to project illusory beasts and ships to overwhelm Republic defenses. He nearly secured victory until his apprentice, Gav Daragon, betrayed him, breaking Sadow's concentration and causing his illusions to dissipate.

Defeated and pursued by Republic forces, Sadow made a daring escape through the twin stars of the Denarii Nova to the jungle moon of Yavin 4. There, he spent decades practicing alchemy and overseeing the construction of massive temple complexes. He placed himself in suspended animation, only to be awakened 600 years later by Freedon Nadd, who learned Sadow's secrets before slaying the ancient Dark Lord.
