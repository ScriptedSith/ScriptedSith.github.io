Ajunta Pall

The First Jen'ari

Once a respected Jedi Master, Ajunta Pall's curiosity led him to the forbidden secrets of Sith alchemy—the power to create and shape life. Deemed an abomination by the Jedi High Council, Pall and his followers were cast out, sparking the century-long conflict known as the Hundred-Year Darkness.

After the final defeat of the Dark Jedi on Corbos, the survivors were stripped of their ranks and exiled into the Unknown Regions. Their wanderings brought them to Korriban, where they encountered the Force-sensitive Sith species. Though King Hakagram Graush resisted, Pall orchestrated his betrayal and personally executed him, using the Dark Jedi's advanced technology and Force mastery to ascend to godhood.

Pall was hailed as the Jen'ari, or "Dark Lord," founding the first Sith Empire. Under his reign, the Empire expanded to worlds like Ziost, which became his capital. Upon his death, his body was returned to Korriban to be entombed in the Valley of the Dark Lords, marking the beginning of a lineage that would haunt the galaxy for millennia.
