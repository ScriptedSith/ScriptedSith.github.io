Imperial Intelligence was the primary intelligence organization that served the Sith Empire during the Great Galactic War. Operating in the shadows, agents of Imperial Intelligence were skilled at manipulation, subterfuge, intimidation and assassination and could be just as deadly as their Sith superiors.
